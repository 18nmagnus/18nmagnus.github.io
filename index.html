SciHound

An open-source app for collecting validation/calibration data for land cover maps.

Find on Google Play Store

Welcome to SciHound for Android.

Developed at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in California, SciHound is an app designed to allow users to easily collect on-site data use to validate and calibrate land cover maps. It is an open-source project geared towards expert users in the industry. SciHound is currently only available for Android on the Google Play Store.

Creating an Account.

SciHound is designed to produce higher-quality data in lower quantities. Thus, our team reviews and individually approves users who want to begin collecting field data. Submit an account request through this website, and we will contact you with further details on registering if you have been approved. All form fields are required.
